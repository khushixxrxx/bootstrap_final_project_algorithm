Upcoming Events

Experience the thrill of the Paris 2024 Olympics. Below is a list of key events to look forward to.

Opening Ceremony

Date: July 26, 2024

Venue: Stade de France, Paris

The grand opening of the Paris 2024 Olympics will feature spectacular performances, the parade of nations, and the lighting of the Olympic flame.

Track & Field

Date: July 27 - August 4, 2024

Venue: Stade de France, Paris

Witness world-class athletes compete in sprinting, long-distance running, hurdles, relays, jumping, and throwing events.

Swimming

Date: July 28 - August 5, 2024

Venue: Aquatics Centre, Paris

Catch the action as swimmers from around the world compete in freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, butterfly, and medley events.

Gymnastics

Date: July 29 - August 7, 2024

Venue: Bercy Arena, Paris

Watch top gymnasts perform routines on the floor, vault, uneven bars, balance beam, and more in this thrilling event.

Closing Ceremony

Date: August 11, 2024

Venue: Stade de France, Paris

The Paris 2024 Olympics will conclude with a spectacular closing ceremony, celebrating the achievements of athletes and the Olympic spirit.
